Desktopify

A new kind of browser experience.
What is Desktopify?

As the name suggests, Desktopify turns websites and web apps into dedicated desktop applications.

Desktopify allows you to view individual websites, or groups of websites, in their own dedicated browser window. This is perfect for reducing tabs in your browser and giving specific websites, such as an email client or web app, their own shortcut on your taskbar, desktop or start menu.

Note that an internet connection is still required.

How does Desktopify Work?
Step 1:

Enter the url of the website you want to desktopify.

Step 2:

Edit settings, such as icon, color and adblocking.

Step 3:

Desktopify will create a start menu icon or desktop icon for your new app.
